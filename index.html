I Told Sunset About You

I Told Sunset About You (known in Thai as แปลรักฉันด้วยใจเธอ; RTGS: Plae Rak Chan Duai Chai Thoe, lit. "translate my love with your heart") is a 2020 Thai romantic drama coming-of-age television series by Nadao Bangkok.
It stars Putthipong Assaratanakul (Billkin) and Krit Amnuaydechkorn (PP), and is being released in two five-episode parts.
The first, directed by Naruebet Kuno, tells the story of the relationship between two secondary schoolboys living in Phuket as they prepare for university admissions, while the second, to be directed by Tossaphon Riantong, will pick up after their entry into university.
They like each other but still they thought as enemies to each other.
